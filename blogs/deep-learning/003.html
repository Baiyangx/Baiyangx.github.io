# text representation: Embeddings & language models

embeddings: (嵌体，由unsupervied-learning训练之后得到的成果，对应一个维度) (weakly-)supervised word embeddings: 对训练结果加上弱限制，比如 按内容的类别产生embeddings， 和按地域类别产生embeddings

not only take care of each phrase, but you also take care of the context.

basic structuring: build some collections, make a serios of phrases more meaningful

  • another important thing: automated named entity recognition (如何自动给entity命名) 然后又引出了“如何多维度地对一个集合进行命名” multidimensional nature of texts

# symbol-based Text representations

  • one-to-one correspondence between text units and representation elements (例如将某个单词转化为一个等价的向量进行表示)
  • Bag-of-words representation 依据出现了哪些词，忽视词序 ，还可以依据 Term Frequency(术语频率TF) and/or inverse document frequency（反转文档频率 IDF

issues： Many dimensions needed, don't reflect semantic similarity

# distributed text representations (so called embeddings) 分布式文本表示

if two words are used or occur in similar context, then they tend to purport similar meanings

we essentially say that the representations of any context unit should be distribute over all vecter dementions

# context-free embeddings

word embedding captures useful properties of word semantics

# feature

  • word similarity: words with similar meanings are embedded closer

  • word analogy: linear relationships between words (king-queen = man-woman)

# model

  • Euclidean space: Word2Vec, GloVe, fastText ?we dont impose any constraints on the learn vectors the vector values can be 。。 word2Vec maximizes the probability of observing a word based on its local context （语境）

  • GloVe： factorizes a global co-occurrence matrix derived from the entire corpus we first build a word-context co-occurrence matrix (sparse, high dimensional) after factorision, we get the low dimensional representation by solving a least-squares(最小二乘法) problem to "recover" the co-occurrence matrix least-squares 向量的最小二乘法用于找到符合给定运算关系的另一个尽可能拟合的向量

  • fastText improves upon Word2Vec by incorporating subword information into word embedding

tri-gram extraction （三元组提取） 给定一段句子文本，抽取句子中的两个实体以及实体之间的关系。（s,p,o）"s的p是o"

fastText allows sharing subword representations across words, since words are represented by the aggregation(集合体) of their n-grams

  • Hyperbolic space: Poincare embeddings sometimes the data can have specific structures that is hard to capture

hyperbolic space（continuous version of tree + naturally equipped to model hierarchical structures） 相当于一个球， 把术语放中间，要辨识的词在边界，就可以建立词语之间的层次关系 基于glove，中心思想都是把高维向量转化为低维表示形式

  • Spherical space:（球面空间） JoSE Directional analysis for text embeddings how to use text embeddings: 思路是利用directional similarity（例如余弦相似性） 来表示两个vector 的相关性

# Deep contextualized embeddings via neural language models

为了解决一次多义问题 the “pretrain-finetune” paradigm（预训练-微调 范式） has become the prominent practice

Firt pre-train language models（PLMS, often based on the Transformer architecture）via self-supervised objectives on large-scale general-domain corpora, then fine-tune them on task-specific data 先是在通用语料库中训练，之后在特定数据集上微调

Based on the pre-training objective/task, can be splited into two types：

  • unidirectional单向(or autoregressive 自动回归)PLM：Predict the next token based on all previous tokens，leveraging single-directional（left to right）contexts(e.g.GPT)
  • Bidirectional (or autoencoding)PLM: Predict masked/corrupted(屏蔽/损坏) tokens based on all other(uncorrupted)tokens, leveraging bidirectional contexts(e.g.,BERT, XLNet, ELECTRA)

  • GPT-style Pre-Training： Generative pre-training

  • ELMO: not based on transformer architecture not deeply bidirectional,

  • BERT: Masked language Modeling BERT leverages a Masked LM learning to introduce real bidirectionality training

    Masked LM: With 15% words randomly masked, the model learns bidirectional contextual information to predict the masked words

    Next Sentence Prediction: learn to predict if the second sentence in the pair is the subsequent sentence in the original document (下一行是否还是后续的句子)
